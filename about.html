Since 2001

Cake, located in Ottawa’s bustling downtown market, offers a total of 22 nut-free cupcake flavours, with twelve available in-store every day. We enjoy suggestions from customers, as we are always baking something new and exciting. Come by and see what's new!

Cake is Ottawa’s first and finest cupcake bakery, and we know how much fun a cupcake can be!

Want to know something we're proud of? We donate thousands of cupcakes every year to local children's charities.

Our cupcakes flavours are chosen by our staff of cupcake artists to make you smile.

All our cupcakes are baked daily from the freshest ingredients. We also offer custom cupcakes, mini cupcakes, cake pops, gluten-free cupcakes made to suit your special celebration.
